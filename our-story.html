Our Story & Beliefs

Rooted in grace, growing in faith, and dedicated to community. Discover the core values and mission that guide everything we do at Bethlehem UMC.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ for the transformation of the world. We seek to live out this mission daily by providing a welcoming space for worship, fostering authentic spiritual growth, and intentionally reaching out to serve our neighbors both locally and globally.

What We Believe

As a community of faith, we are grounded in the Wesleyan tradition of grace. We believe in a theology that emphasizes God's unconditional love, the power of community, and an active faith that expresses itself through acts of compassion, justice, and devotion.
